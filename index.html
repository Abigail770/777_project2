About the Park

Devil's Lake, located in Baraboo WI in Sauk County, is Wisconsin's most visted state park. Its steep bluffs offer spectacular views of the lake below, which is great for fishing and canoeing. In addition to boating, swimming, and hiking, the park's cliffs draw rock climbers and boulderers from across the Midwest.

The bluffs surrounding Devil's Lake are a part of the Baraboo Range, one of the oldest rock outcrops on the continent.
